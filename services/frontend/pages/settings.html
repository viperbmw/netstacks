Netbox Device Filters

Filter which devices are shown from Netbox. Add multiple filters to narrow down your device list. View Examples

Common Filter Examples:

  • tag=production - Devices with "production" tag
  • name__ic=router - Devices with "router" in name (case-insensitive)
  • site=datacenter-1 - Devices at specific site
  • status=active - Only active devices
  • role=access-switch - Devices with specific role
  • manufacturer=cisco - Filter by manufacturer

Filters use Netbox API syntax. Multiple filters are combined with AND logic.
